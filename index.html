A little about me.

I reside in San Antonio, Texas, with my husband and two girls. I have worked as a receptionist, secretary, loan processor, and in data entry positions. In 2019, my dad started a business. He recruited my help; first task... create a website. Simple enough, but I had no idea where to begin. Since then my knowledge has grown and I have discovered a love for front-end web development. Now I have made a full switch. I'm looking forward to creating elegant and clean codes with my eye for detail and organizational skills.
